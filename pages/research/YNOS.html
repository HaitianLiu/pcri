YNOS DATABASE ON THE INDIAN START-UP ECOSYSTEM

The Private Capital Research Institute has a partnership with YNOS, a digital meta-information platform, to provide our researchers with access to high quality information on start-ups, founders, angels, incubators, and venture capital investors in India. The YNOS data repository has been developed with the technical support of the Centre for Research on Start-ups and Risk Financing (CREST), Indian Institute of Technology Madras . YNOS database includes all start-ups and not just funded start-ups and researchers can use a very large spectrum of variables encompassing start-up background, founder demographics, start-up funding and performance, angel investors demographics and investing trends, incubators and their start-up cohorts, and so on. For more information about this database please visit here.

HOW TO ACCESS:

You will need to apply for access to the YNOS database. Please submit a 1-2 paragraph description of your research topic. If approved, you will receive a login to the YNOS online database.
