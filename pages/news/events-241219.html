PCRI –TAU VENTURE CAPITAL SUMMIT

The Evolution of Israeli Institutional VC Investment

Thursday, December 19, 2024


The Private Capital Research Institute (PCRI) and the Coller School of Management at Tel Aviv University hosted the "PCRI–TAU Venture Capital Summit," a landmark event that brought together thought leaders, policymakers, and industry experts to explore the transformative journey of Israeli institutional venture capital investment.

The summit featured insightful discussions on innovation during times of uncertainty, the future of Israel’s high-tech sector, and the evolving role of venture capital amid geopolitical challenges. Notable speakers included Naftali Bennett, former Prime Minister of Israel, and Prof. Josh Lerner from Harvard Business School, alongside key figures from prominent firms such as Bessemer, Blackstone, and Pitango. Through panels and academic sessions, participants examined the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities shaping Israel’s venture capital ecosystem, offering a roadmap for future growth and innovation.
