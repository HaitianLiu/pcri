Chinese Private Markets Today

A workshop hosted by the PCRI and the PBC School of Finance, Tsinghua University

Tuesday, October 24, 2023


The "Chinese Private Markets Today" workshop, jointly presented by the Private Capital Research Institute (PCRI) and Tsinghua University, provided a unique platform for experts to engage in deep conversations about the dynamic contours of China's private markets and the promising investment prospects that are emerging within this sphere.
